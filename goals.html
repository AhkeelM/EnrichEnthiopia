Goals

The project’s goals are to foster a more developed, equitable, and modernized private sector in Ethiopia by creating opportunities and providing resources in the form of capital and education for small business owners and low income potential business owners. The creation of our online trading platform, as well as we aim to introduce a new culture of mobile banking, e-commerce, and trade into the country’s economy. This will be revolutionary for the market, trade, and entrepreneurship, and allows for exposure to a larger Ethiopian market that was not available prior to this. This will propel the Ethiopian economy into the future. We also hope that by proving that low income people and entrepreneurs can be trusted with loans, access to loans will become increasingly more accessible for them in the future.
