Activities

With the $100 million advancements we aim to promote more sustainable development of the private sector by investing in Ethiopians impoverished entrepreneurial community, specifically women. We are striving to provide resources in the form of capital to people who would otherwise not have access to loans or other forms of capital.










Project Activities

Establishing a community banking system called The Peopleâ€™s Bank of Ethiopia in Addis Ababa that will give loans to small businesses. Our target is mainly women entrepreneurs, but anyone can receive business loans. The bank will set up a simple mobile credit and banking system that will be easily compatible with the e-commerce platform. There will be a 0.5% fee on all transactions to generate revenue for the bank.

Business and management training programs for small business owners and potential entrepreneurs that will include a mentorship program for small business owners with business owners who are already established in the private sector. Programs will include finance and budgeting management, digital platform strategy, value creation and profitability, and business management. These programs will be provided in partnership with Addis Ababa University and funded by Enrich Ethiopia

Creating an online platform for an e-commerce marketplace. The e-commerce platform will also have small fees to begin selling products and services as well as the same 0.5% fee on transactions.
