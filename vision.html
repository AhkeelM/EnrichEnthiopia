Our Vision

Enriching and planting the seeds in bussinesses to increase the number of private sectors in Ethiopia.







Project Design

Needs and Assets Assessment: We will need an office space to operate out of, as well as employees with finance and business backgrounds to carry out our project. We will also need employees with public relations and communications backgrounds to help us connect and interact with the banks, governmental bodies, and colleges that we intend to work with and the bigger business owners that will be involved in the mentorship program. We will need to build an online trade platform that requires web developers to create.

Stakeholder Analysis: Stakeholders include local banks that will be servicing the loans with the seed money, colleges that we will partner with to provide courses and training, Ethio Telecommunications which will be both a stakeholder and a recipient of investments, and the established entrepreneurs that are in the mentorship program.
